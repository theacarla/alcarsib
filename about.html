About Us

A multimedia company founded in 2022, alcarsib is one of the leading companies on artwork creation. We focus on customer satusfaction and quality works in each request. Our founder, Althea Carla Sibulo, hence, alcarsib, is a production designer at FEU Theater Guild and currently a second-year student at FEU Institute of Technology. Unlike other art commissions, alcarsibs ranges to art styles you cannot imagine. It allows customers to freely request without any limits, unleashing their full creativity.
